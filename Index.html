Editorial Internship Portfolio
Taylor & Francis Group, Philadelphia, PA

This portfolio contains highlights of the work I completed during my internship in the Editorial Department of Taylor & Francis Group's Philadelphia office. The internship spanned late October 2018 to early March 2019.

List of Projects

Competitor Analysis

Investigated commercial competitors, digital archives, and open access resources to determine the viability of a new Taylor & Francis digital offering. Work involved cross-departmental collaboration with Digital Resources.

Diversity Research

Researched the editorial board makeup of competitors of Taylor & Francis's journals and analyzed their relative diversity. Work required knowledge of the environmental sciences publishing landscape, use of analytical tools such as Web of Science, and proficiency with Microsoft Excel.

Journal Market Review

Examined the publishing field for journals on the topics of nuclear waste management and nuclear security. Project required the use of analytical tools such as Journal Citation Reports and SCOPUS.

Altmetrics

Used Altmetrics to determine journals' online footprints measured by attention on social media and other web platforms. Multiple projects required knowledge of online impact measurements based on quality and quantity of mentions.

Publisher's Reports

Composed comprehensive reports on journals based their on usage data, impact factor, and a variety of other performance benchmarks. Multiple projects required analytical skills, information synthesis, and attention to detail.
